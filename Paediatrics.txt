   **Answer: D) Clobazam (or diazepam; given answer iii.d)**

**Case 19 (CMC):** 2 month old infant with cough and breathlessness, suck-rest-suck cycle, pansystolic murmur at lower left sternal border.
   i) Most likely acyanotic heart disease? a) ASD b) VSD c) PDA d) Coarctation
   **Answer: b) VSD**
   ii) Nadas major criteria include? a) Systolic murmur grade 2 or more b) Clubbing c) Congestive heart failure d) Abnormal ECG
   **Answer: c) Congestive heart failure (and cyanosis, diastolic murmur, etc.)**
   iii) First line management in congestive cardiac failure? a) Digoxin b) Diuretics c) ACE inhibitors d) Vasodilators
   **Answer: b) Diuretics**
   iv) High volume collapsing pulse is a feature of? a) ASD b) VSD c) PDA d) TOF
   **Answer: c) PDA**
   v) In which condition is left atrium NOT enlarged? a) VSD b) Aorto-pulmonary window c) ASD d) PDA
   **Answer: c) ASD (left atrium not enlarged in isolated ASD)**
